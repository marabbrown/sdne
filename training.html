Airscent Training/Searching Time Line

  • Attend 9 trainings within 3 months

  • Apply to SDNE for membership

  • Acceptance as member with 6 months probation

  • Written essay test within 6 months of acceptance

  • CPR certification within 6 months of acceptance

  • FUNSAR or equivalent

  • Complete SDNE navigation test within 6 months of acceptance

  • Crime scene preservation training

  • First aid certificate

  • Apply for Field Level Status (non dog handler) providing requirements are met for
    MASSARDF and/or RI EMA certifications.

  • Canine Good Citizen certificate, or equivalent

  • Complete SDNE required reading list

  • Submit minimum 6 months training logs

  • Apply and complete the night hasty test

  • Apply and complete the 40 acre field competency evaluation

  • Canine first aid certificate

  • Physical fitness hike (3 miles in 2 hours)- self certification

  • Apply and complete the SDNE Level 1 40 acre Daytime test

  • Request to start MASSARDF testing (hasty & 40-day)

  • Request to take RI 40 acre certification test

  • Apply and complete the SDNE Level 2 40 acre Night test

  • Request to take MASSARDF 40 acre night test

  • Attempt the Level 3 160 acre field test

  • Request to take MASSARDF 160 acre field test
